Earthquakes can strike without warning. Being prepared can help keep you and your loved ones safe.

During an Earthquake

Drop: Get down on your hands and knees to protect yourself from being knocked over.
Cover: Take cover under a sturdy table or piece of furniture. Protect your head and neck.
Hold On: Hold on to your shelter until the shaking stops. Stay away from windows and glass.
