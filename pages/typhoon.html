Typhoons can bring destructive winds and heavy rainfall. Proper preparation is essential for safety.

During a Typhoon

Stay Inside: Remain indoors and away from windows and glass doors.
Be Prepared: Keep your emergency kit ready with food, water, and essential supplies.
Stay Informed: Monitor weather updates and follow evacuation orders if given.
